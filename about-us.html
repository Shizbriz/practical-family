About the family

Welcome to the family. We are basically Oluwaseyi Ogunrinde, a couple of her favorites and just some people whose name she remembers and whose picture she can find.

Oluwaseyi Ogunrinde is a teacher, post graduate Microbiology student and a tech enthusiast. She also loves food very much and would love to eat a wide variety of foods from different countries before she dies.

Dwayne Michael Carter is a musician whose speciality is hip hop, particularly rap. He started his rap career very early in life and is popularly known as Lil Wayne. He made away with the D in his name as Dwayne is also his father's name and he has cut his father off from his life for abandoning him when he was 2.

Judy Sheindlin is a television judge better known as Judge Judy which is also the name of her television show. She is a no nonsense judge who is very blunt and judges some small real life cases on television. Her style has been copied unsuccessfully by other TV shows.

Lee Min Ho is a Korean actor who gained popularity for his role as Gun Jun Pyio in the series Boys Over Flowers. This series not only introduced Lee Min Ho to the world, it also introduced Korean movies to Nigeria. Lee Min Ho went on to star in other Korean movies like City Hunter.

Angelina Jolie is an American actress popular for her roles as a badass. She is married to American actor Bradd Pitt and is the one to root for in any movie she stars in.

Kevin Hart is a short black American comedian. He stars in movies and does skits which are usually a lot funnier than his stand up comedy. Other than for comedy, he is also popular for cheating on his wife.

Martha Stewart is a billionaire businesswoman and a home maker. She has a website where she shares recipes of really delicious looking food. She has been to jail for felony after being caught in tax fraud
